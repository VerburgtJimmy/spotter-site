Privacy Policy

Last updated 15 June 2026

Spotter is built to keep your data yours.

On-device storage

Your workouts, sessions, and history are stored locally on your device and synced through your own private iCloud. Spotter has no account system and does not transmit your data to me or any third party.

No tracking, no ads, no analytics

Spotter does not track you across apps or websites and contains no advertising or analytics SDKs.

Apple Health

With your permission, Spotter writes completed workouts to Apple Health and can read body metrics such as weight and height to personalize features. This data is processed on your device and is never sent to me.

Apple Intelligence

On supported devices, workout generation and adaptation run on-device using Apple’s Foundation Models. Your data is not sent to a server.

iCloud sync

Your workouts and history sync through your own private iCloud account under Apple’s privacy terms. The data lives in your iCloud, not on my servers, and I never see it.

Purchases

In-app purchases are handled by Apple. I do not receive your payment details.
