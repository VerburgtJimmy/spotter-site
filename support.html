Before you write

A few things that solve most issues:

  • Apple Health not updating? Open the Health app → Sharing → Apps → Spotter, and make sure the categories you want are enabled.
  • AI features missing? Workout generation and "Adapt for today" need an iPhone with Apple Intelligence. On other devices they're simply hidden.
  • Watch or widgets out of sync? Make sure both devices are on the same iCloud account and connected, then reopen the app.
  • Restoring a purchase? Go to the paywall and tap Restore Purchases while signed in to the App Store account you bought with.

When reporting a bug, it helps to include your iPhone model and iOS version, and a quick description of what you expected versus what happened.
