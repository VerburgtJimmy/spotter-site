How do I move my data from another app?

In Settings → Import, choose your app (Strong, Hevy, or Jefit), export a backup from that app, and open it in Spotter. Your sessions, records, and routines come across.

Where is my data stored?

On your device, synced through your own iCloud. There is no account and nothing is stored on my servers. See the Privacy Policy.

What does Spotter Pro unlock?

Unlimited custom workouts. The free tier keeps two, with unlimited logging. Pro is a one-time €24.99, or €1.99/month.

How do I back up or export my workouts?

In Settings you can export a full backup file at any time. Your iCloud also keeps your data in sync across your devices.
